Darjeeling is one of the most popular tea gardens in the world. It is a tourist destination with a rich history and culture. Darjeeling is one of the most popular tea-producing areas in India. It has a rich history and culture. The tea industry is one of the main sources of income for people living in the region. The popularity of Darjeeling tea is not only due to its unique flavor, but also because it is one of the cheapest teas in the world and the most popular tea in Bangladesh. When it comes to must-see attractions or things to do in Darjeeling, there are a few that come to mind. Firstly, the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway is a must-see. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a narrow gauge railway that runs from Darjeeling to Ghum and is one of the oldest mountain railways in India. It’s definitely worth a visit! Secondly, Tiger Hill Sunrise Point is another must-see. Located at an altitude of 2,590m, it’s the highest point in Darjeeling and offers stunning views of the sunrise over the Himalayas. Finally, the Ghum Monastery is also worth a visit. It’s the largest Tibetan Buddhist monastery in India and home to the second-tallest statue of the Buddha in the world.
